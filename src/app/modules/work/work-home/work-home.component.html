Research Vision

My research is driven by a single question: how do we make AI systems worthy of trust? At a time when AI is reshaping every critical domain — healthcare, law, finance, national security — the cost of opaque, brittle, and vulnerable models is no longer theoretical. My work sits at the intersection of Explainable AI, Adversarial Robustness, and AI Security, with a singular goal: to make intelligent systems not just powerful, but accountable, resilient, and safe for the communities that depend on them. I believe responsible AI is not a constraint on progress — it is the condition for it.

Before research, I spent 4+ years as a Software Engineer building production-grade systems used by thousands — across telemedicine platforms in South Asia and international tax research tools used across Europe. That background shapes how I think: not just as a scientist asking what is possible, but as an engineer who knows what it takes to build things that must actually work in the world.
